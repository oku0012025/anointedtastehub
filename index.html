Our Story

Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded in 2025, Anointed Taste Hub brings authentic Nigerian flavors to your table. Our master chef, Anointing Ikeru, has perfected traditional Nigerian recipes passed down through generations, combining time-honored cooking methods with modern presentation.

We source only the freshest local ingredients daily, combining traditional Nigerian techniques with contemporary presentation to create a dining experience that honors our rich culinary heritage while appealing to modern tastes.
